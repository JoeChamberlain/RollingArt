About

Rolling Art began as a passion for detailing and turned into a full time business venture. The company founder, Ashton Hawes, took his passion for cars to a higher level by honing his detailing skills and challenging himself on difficult restoration projects. Rolling Art offers a variety of these trained skills depending on the level of service needed for each vehicle.

I am dedicated to restoring and maintaining my customers cars that we as enthusiasts see as Rolling Art.

owner, Ashton Hawes
